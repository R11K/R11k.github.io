Farm in a Box

4 image slot(s) — replace placeholders with your photos

Description

Farm in a Box is a germination grow kit designed to make hands-on plant science accessible in classrooms. The core unit is laser-cut from bass wood and houses an integrated LED grow light. A second version is in development that adds a microcontroller and sensors, letting students monitor their plants’ conditions over time.

The project spanned mechanical design, prototyping, and production planning — taking an educational product from invention through a registered design.
